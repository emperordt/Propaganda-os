Impact — How big the effect could be if it works.
10 breakthrough · 7-9 solid · 4-6 average · 1-3 limited
Confidence — Certainty we can execute well.
10 done before · 7-9 clear path · 4-6 unknowns · 1-3 many unknowns
Ease — Production simplicity.
10 single take · 7-9 simple setup · 4-6 multi-shot · 1-3 complex
ICE = (I + C + E) ÷ 3
